About & Help

Welcome to Kelime, your personal vocabulary learning assistant.

How It Works

This app is designed to help you build your vocabulary from the texts you read. You can add any text source—an article, a book chapter, or just a snippet of text—and the app will automatically extract the unique words for you to learn.

Spaced Repetition

The review system uses a Spaced Repetition System (SRS). This is a learning technique that schedules reviews at increasing intervals. When you know a word, the app will wait longer before showing it to you again. If you forget a word, it will be shown to you more frequently until you master it. This method is highly effective for long-term retention.

Manually Adding Words

If you already know a word and don't want it to appear in your review queue, you can use the "Mark a Word as Known" tool on the dashboard. Simply type the word and submit it.
